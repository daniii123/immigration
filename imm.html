Home

Our mission is to bring awareness to the challenges faced by immigrants, as well as to make it easier to find information on which rights correspond to every situation. This website will help many understand the citizenship/residential process in more depth. Younger people such as teens will be able to access this information faster without having to go through piles of articles. Immigration is a very controversial topic and plays a very important role in our country and in our future, therefore it is extremely important for everyone to be aware. We also want this website to be a place where anyone will feel welcomed and be informed for protection.
